Our Story

Born in the hills, kept in their purity.

Meghyra began with a simple idea: that the hills of Meghalaya already make food the right way, and the least we could do was carry it to your table without getting in the way.

We work directly with farming families and native communities across Meghalaya — no middlemen, no rushed harvests, no shortcuts taken in the name of shelf life. Honey is drawn from wild hives and left raw. Turmeric is stone-ground the way it always has been. Rice is grown from heirloom seed, not hybrid stock bred for yield alone.

Every batch is small, seasonal, and traceable to the farm it came from. What you receive is close to what the hills actually grow — nothing added, nothing refined away.
